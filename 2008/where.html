Exhibition venue
 
Ex-Chiesa Metodista - via Lagrange 13, 10122 Torino - Map

Central Via Lagrange hides a true architectural jewel, almost unheard-of. A tiny courtyard leads us to a beautiful church, built at the end of XIX Century.
A recent renovation by architect Maurizio Zucca turned it into a place for arts: white walls and glass, old and contemporary make a perfect mix between new and recent elements.
An open space marked by four columns and a bright skylight.
Not the average white box, but a unique and striking place for arts.
