Thursday 1 June 2006 - h 18,30 - 32 Dicembre
free entry
Exhibition opening

The opening of the exhibition will be at the 32 Dicembre Association's site, present the artists invited.
It will be also presented the exhibition's catalogue, with an introduction of Domenico Quaranta.
After the event, the exhibit will be relocate to the Galleria Allegretti Contemporanea, via San Francesco D'Assisi 14.

The artists taking part to the exhibition are:
Marius Watz
Alessandro Capozzo
Limiteazero
Fabio Franchino

 
Friday 2 June 2006 - h 18,30 - AB+
free entry
Conference

Moderator: Domenico Quaranta

Round table for discussing and understanding the present Italian and foreign scene of new creative and experimental methodologies which employ electronical and informational systems.
The artists are invited to present their methodologies and strategies, by showing some published and unpublished works, with the aim of understanding the common outline and, subsequently, the expressive potentialities of the proposed works.

 
Friday 2 June 2006 - from h 22,30 - AB+
free entry
C.STEM party

Sound: Elettrolebowki

The evening will give the possibility to see many works by the artists, since the projections will be distributed all around the site. People willing to know more about software art's technics and scene, will also have the possibility to meet the artist in an informal way.
